Derniere mise a jour : 22 fevrier 2026

1. Introduction

PIVOT ("Property Intelligent Visit Offline Tool") is a mobile application developed by FlowForges designed to assist real estate professionals during property visits. This Privacy Policy describes how PIVOT handles your data.

Our core principle: PIVOT is an offline-first, zero-knowledge application. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on our servers. We have no servers.

2. Data Controller

FlowForges
Contact: Hamsterjoevial@outlook.fr

3. Data We Collect

We collect NO personal data. PIVOT operates entirely on your device.

The following data is created and stored locally on your device only:

  • Visit data: Property names, addresses, notes, checklist ratings, and comments you enter during property visits.
  • Media files: Photos, audio recordings, and videos you capture during visits. These files are stored in the app's private storage on your device.
  • App preferences: Language preference, onboarding completion status, Pro purchase status. Stored securely using your device's secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).

None of this data is ever sent to us or to any third-party server unless you explicitly choose to export or back up your data (see Section 5).

4. Device Permissions

PIVOT requests the following device permissions, all used exclusively for their stated purpose:

PermissionPurposeWhen Requested
CameraTake photos and record videos during property visitsWhen you tap the photo/video capture button
MicrophoneRecord audio notes during visitsWhen you tap the audio recording button
Photo LibrarySave exported photosWhen you export visit data

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device's Settings. PIVOT will continue to function with reduced capability if permissions are denied.

5. Cloud Backup (Optional)

PIVOT offers optional cloud backup to:

  • Google Drive: Using your own Google account
  • Microsoft OneDrive: Using your own Microsoft account

Important details about cloud backup:

  • Cloud backup is never automatic. You must explicitly tap a button to initiate each backup.
  • Data is uploaded directly from your device to your own cloud storage account. It does not pass through our servers.
  • We do not have access to your cloud storage accounts or the files you upload.
  • Authentication tokens for Google/Microsoft are stored securely on your device using platform-native secure storage.
  • You can disconnect your cloud accounts at any time from within the app.
  • Backup files are stored in a "PIVOT Backups" folder in your cloud storage. You can delete them at any time.

6. In-App Purchases

PIVOT offers an optional one-time purchase ("PIVOT Pro") processed through:

  • Apple App Store (iOS) via StoreKit
  • Google Play Store (Android) via Google Play Billing

Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple/Google. We do not collect or store any payment information. Your Pro status is stored locally on your device.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Period.

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are included in PIVOT.
  • No advertising SDKs are included.
  • No data is sent to any server (we have none).
  • The only network requests PIVOT makes are for cloud backup (Section 5) and in-app purchase verification (Section 6), both initiated exclusively by you.

8. Data Export

You can export your visit data at any time in multiple formats (ZIP, PDF, CSV, JSON). Exported data belongs entirely to you. When you share exported data via your device's share sheet, it is handled by the sharing app you choose — PIVOT has no control over or visibility into that process.

9. Data Retention

All data is stored on your device. We retain nothing.

  • Local data: Persists until you delete the visit or uninstall the app.
  • iOS Keychain data: May persist after app uninstallation (this is standard iOS behavior for Keychain items).
  • Cloud backup data: Persists in your cloud storage until you delete it manually.

10. Data Security

  • All sensitive preferences are stored using platform-native secure storage:
    • iOS: Keychain Services (hardware-backed encryption)
    • Android: Android Keystore (hardware-backed encryption)
  • Visit data and media files are stored in the app's private sandbox, inaccessible to other apps.
  • No data is transmitted over the network except for optional cloud backup (encrypted via HTTPS/TLS).

11. Children's Privacy

PIVOT is designed for real estate professionals and is not intended for use by children under 16. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

12. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we have no access to it, you exercise your data rights directly:

  • Right to access: Open the app to view all your data.
  • Right to export: Use the export feature to get all your data in standard formats.
  • Right to delete: Delete individual visits or uninstall the app to remove all data.
  • Right to rectification: Edit your visit data directly in the app.

We cannot assist with data access requests because we do not have your data.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly. Continued use of PIVOT after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
